About FleetGuide.in

Your trusted partner in fleet management and transport optimization

Who We Are

FleetGuide.in is India's leading online platform dedicated to empowering fleet owners, transport managers, and commercial vehicle drivers with the tools and knowledge they need to optimize their operations. Founded in 2026, we recognized a critical gap in the Indian transport industry: the lack of accessible, practical, and reliable fleet management resources.

We are a team of transport industry professionals and technology enthusiasts who understand the challenges faced by fleet operators across India. From individual truck owners to large logistics companies, we serve thousands of users every month with free tools, comprehensive guides, and expert insights.

Our Mission

To democratize fleet management by providing free, accessible, and high-quality tools and information that help Indian transport businesses reduce costs, improve efficiency, and maintain compliance with regulations.
