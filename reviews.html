Commercial Vehicle Reviews

Expert reviews and comparisons of popular fleet vehicles

Tata Ace Gold

Overview

The Tata Ace Gold is India's most popular mini-truck, perfect for intra-city deliveries and small businesses.

Specifications

  • Payload: 750 kg
  • Engine: 700cc diesel
  • Power: 16 HP
  • Mileage: 18-20 km/L
  • Price: ₹3.99 - 4.8 Lakhs

Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • Excellent fuel efficiency
  • Low maintenance costs
  • Easy to maneuver in city traffic
  • Wide service network

Cons:

  • Limited to 750 kg payload
  • Not suitable for highways

Mahindra Bolero Pickup

Overview

Rugged and reliable pickup truck ideal for construction, agriculture, and small goods transport.

Specifications

  • Payload: 1,250 kg
  • Engine: 2.5L diesel
  • Power: 63 HP
  • Mileage: 14-16 km/L
  • Price: ₹7.41 - 9.97 Lakhs

Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • Strong build quality
  • Good for rough terrain
  • Higher payload capacity
  • Comfortable cabin

Cons:

  • Higher fuel consumption
  • Expensive compared to mini-trucks

Ashok Leyland Dost+

Overview

Light commercial vehicle with excellent load-carrying capacity and fuel economy.

Specifications

  • Payload: 1,550 kg
  • Engine: 1.5L diesel
  • Power: 80 HP
  • Mileage: 17-19 km/L
  • Price: ₹7.75 - 8.95 Lakhs

Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • High payload capacity
  • Good fuel efficiency
  • i-GEN6 technology
  • 5-year warranty

Cons:

  • Limited service network in rural areas
  • Higher initial cost

Eicher Pro 2049

Overview

Light truck ideal for inter-city transport with excellent reliability and performance.

Specifications

  • Payload: 4,100 kg
  • Engine: 2.98L diesel
  • Power: 95 HP
  • Mileage: 12-14 km/L
  • Price: ₹12.82 - 14.75 Lakhs

Pros & Cons

Pros:

  • High payload for its class
  • Comfortable for long distance
  • Strong engine performance
  • Good resale value

Cons:

  • Higher purchase price
  • Maintenance costs higher than smaller vehicles

Choosing the Right Vehicle

For Intra-City Delivery:

  • Recommended: Tata Ace Gold, Mahindra Jeeto
  • Reason: Excellent mileage, easy maneuverability, low running costs

For Inter-City Transport:

  • Recommended: Ashok Leyland Dost+, Eicher Pro 2049
  • Reason: Higher payload, better highway performance, comfortable cabins

For Construction/Agriculture:

  • Recommended: Mahindra Bolero Pickup, Tata Yodha
  • Reason: Rugged build, handles rough terrain, versatile loading options
