How to Use Fleet Calculators

Distance Calculator

Calculate accurate distance between two GPS coordinates using the Haversine formula. Perfect for route planning and distance estimation. Enter latitude and longitude for both points to get distance in kilometers and miles.

Speed & Time Calculators

Calculate speed from distance and time, or calculate time needed for a journey. Essential for planning deliveries, estimating arrival times, and optimizing routes.

Bearing Calculator

Find the compass direction between two points. Useful for navigation and understanding route directions. Results given in degrees (0-360¬∞) and cardinal directions (N, NE, E, etc.).

Coordinate Converters

Convert between different coordinate formats (decimal degrees, degrees-minutes-seconds). Essential when working with different GPS devices and mapping systems.

Unit Converters

Convert between metric and imperial units - kilometers to miles, km/h to mph, km/L to mpg. Useful when working with international routes or vehicles.

Route Planning

Calculate total distance for multi-stop routes. Plan efficient delivery routes and estimate total journey distances accurately.

ETA Calculator

Calculate estimated time of arrival including break times. Perfect for informing customers of delivery times and planning driver schedules.

Common Use Cases

  • Route Planning: Use distance and time calculators to plan optimal routes
  • Fuel Estimation: Calculate distance first, then use fuel calculator
  • Driver Scheduling: Use ETA calculator to plan driver shifts
  • International Routes: Convert units between metric and imperial
  • GPS Navigation: Convert coordinates between different formats
  • Area Coverage: Calculate delivery radius and coverage areas
