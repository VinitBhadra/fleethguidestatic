How to Use Our Fleet Calculators

Our calculators are designed to be simple yet powerful. Here's how to get the most accurate results:

Fuel Cost Calculator:

Perfect for trip planning and budgeting. Enter the distance you plan to travel, current fuel price (check your local pump), and your vehicle's actual mileage. The calculator instantly shows fuel required and total cost. Use this before every trip to avoid budget surprises.

Trip Cost Calculator:

Calculate the complete cost of any trip including all expenses. Besides fuel, add driver payment, toll charges, and any other costs. This helps you:

  • Quote accurate prices to clients
  • Understand true trip profitability
  • Compare different routes by total cost
  • Budget for long-distance hauls

Cost Per KM Calculator:

Track operational efficiency over time. Calculate your cost per kilometer to:

  • Compare different vehicles in your fleet
  • Identify which routes are most profitable
  • Benchmark against industry standards (‚Çπ8-15/km for most trucks)
  • Spot when maintenance or fuel issues increase costs

Tips for Accurate Calculations:

  • Use current prices: Fuel prices change frequently, use today's rate
  • Account for load: Heavy loads reduce mileage by 10-20%
  • Include all costs: Don't forget toll, parking, or permit fees
  • Track actual mileage: Highway vs city driving affects results
  • Regular updates: Re-calculate when fuel prices change significantly
